The Resource Name section includes the resource name, the RRID (Research Resource Identifier), and the proper citation of the resource to allow researchers to easily copy for citing in a paper or grant.

What are RRIDs (Research Resource Identifiers)? RRIDs are are unique identifiers for research resources. RRIDs provide uniformity in regards to the citation and tracking of resources, and have been adopted by many publishers and agencies involved in reproducibility in the scientific community.
